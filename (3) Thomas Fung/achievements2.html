Aberdeen Centre

Aberdeen Centre is a shopping mall in Richmond, BC. The original building was built in 1989 and it contained 50 to 75 stores. The name of the mall referred to a part of Hong Kong, known as Aberdeen. In 2006, it was bought by Thomas Fung's Fairchild Group. They built studios for Fairchild TV and radio in the mall. Over time, Aberdeen Centre has risen up to become one of the largest and most well-known asian malls in Vancouver. Next section.

Fairchild Group

The Fairchild Group is a Canadian business conglomerate, with headquarters in Vancouver, BC. The group operates various media properties, such as TV channels and radio networks. Fairchild Group is also involved in film production, real estste development, retail, telecommunications, and wholesale trade. In the stock market, Fairchild has a market cap of 2 billion dollars.
